DESCRIPTION
This competency equips you with a deep understanding of platform businesses, including their functions and core principles. You'll learn to conceptualize, research, analyze, and develop a comprehensive business plan for your own platform. Whether you aspire to create your own platform or build a product strategy, this competency will broaden your business acumen and provide a holistic view of organizational success.
