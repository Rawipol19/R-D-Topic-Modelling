Details for Competency HCD-405 (Sound Design)

IDENTITY CODE: SOUND_DESIGN_4
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 4 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Students will identify, generate, and compose all four of the key audio inputs for a digital media product/game. These include:

MX: Music within gaming operates within unique constraints, including in-game triggers and composing original soundtracks which can play continuously and shift cleanly based on player behavior.
SFX: Generate all of the required active sounds within a gaming environment (i.e. item collection notification, weapon impacts, etc.).
Foley: Apply atmospheric and player-centric ambience, such as footfalls and other environmental context.
VX: Learn the basics of voice acting and locution, as well as how to record and edit dialogue audio based on characterization.
