Details for Competency SEC-201 (Data Privacy, Security and Integrity)

IDENTITY CODE: DATA_PRIVACY_4
REQUIRED? true
CREDITS: 4 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Data is the most valuable asset in information systems. Most of the time, data contains personal information that could be harmful to its owner if exposed. In this competency, we will learn various techniques to preserve data privacy, security, and integrity properly and effectively.
