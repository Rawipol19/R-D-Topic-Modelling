Details for Competency SCI-106 (Dynamics: explain motion (Physics I))

IDENTITY CODE: DYNAMICS_3
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 3 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
This competency provides an introduction to the fundamental principles and concepts of kinematics, the branch of mechanics that deals with the motion of objects. This course covers topics such as displacement, velocity, acceleration, and the equations of motion.
