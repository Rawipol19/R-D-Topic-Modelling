Details for Competency HCD-201 (Ethics in computer engineering)

IDENTITY CODE: ETHICS_CE_2
REQUIRED? true
CREDITS: 2 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Computer engineering and AI offer incredible capabilities to both individuals and institutions, but with great capabilities come great responsibilities. Ethics in computer engineering covers a wide range of topics, including privacy, automation & employment, big data collection and analysis, safety, and best practices in design. This includes a commitment to the ACM Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct.
