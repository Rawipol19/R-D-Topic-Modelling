Details for Competency SCI-110 (Magnetism (Physics II))

IDENTITY CODE: MAGNETISM_4
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 4 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
This course delves into the fundamental principles and applications of magnetism, exploring its role in the natural world and technological applications. Students will start by studying the origins of magnetism, including magnetic fields and the behavior of magnetic materials. The course will cover topics such as the laws governing magnetic fields, electromagnetic induction, and the interaction of magnetic fields with electric currents.
