IDENTITY CODE: URD_PROJ_4_9
REQUIRED? true
CREDITS: 9 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
In URD-201 and URD-202, student teams work with external stakeholders from organizations that are dedicated to solving societal problems. These could be non-profits, non-governmental organizations, or corporate-social-responsibility units within for-profit companies. URD-201 and URD-202 challenge student teams not only to design and build a solution to the stakeholders' problem but to do so considering practical constraints (including financial, technical and knowledge-oriented) faced by the stakeholder organizations.
