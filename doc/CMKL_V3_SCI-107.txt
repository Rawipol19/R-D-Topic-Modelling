Details for Competency SCI-107 (Energy and Momentum (Physics I))

IDENTITY CODE: ENERGY_MOM_3
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 3 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
It provides an introduction to the fundamental principles and concepts of energy and momentum in the context of classical mechanics. This course covers topics such as work, kinetic and potential energy, the conservation of energy, linear momentum, and the conservation of momentum.
