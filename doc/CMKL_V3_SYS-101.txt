Details for Competency SYS-101 (Operating Systems)

IDENTITY CODE: OS_UNIX_4
REQUIRED? true

DESCRIPTION
This competency provides an overview of operating systems responsibilities & required  mechanisms for multitasking and resource management of a computer system. This competency will focus on the virtualization abstraction and concurrency aspect of operating systems.
