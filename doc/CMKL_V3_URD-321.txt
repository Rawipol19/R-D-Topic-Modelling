Details for Competency URD-321 (Industrial Internship (1))

IDENTITY CODE: INTERNSHIP_1_9
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 9 (Graded)
OPTION GROUP: Third Year R\&D Semester 1

DESCRIPTION
This competency is intended to give students practical experience working on-site in a company or research institution as part of an existing team. Students will fill a specific role determined by their supervisor at the host institution and will participate in the day-to-day activities of the institution, working on assigned tasks and contribution to their team of colleagues. Internships usually last for three months (one semester) but may be extended.
