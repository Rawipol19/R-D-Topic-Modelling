Details for Competency HCD-502 (Interaction Design & Experience Design)

IDENTITY CODE: INTERACT_DESIGN_10
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 10 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Designing for user interaction (UI) and optimizing the user experience (UX) is a central pillar in user-centered design thinking, as it pertains to how users will engage with digital content and what that engagement will feel like experientially. This course will identify best practices within both UX and UI, and demonstrate case studies and examples to emphasize how these best practices are used in real-world applications.
