Details for Competency SEN-403 (Software Organization Maturity and Continuous Improvement)

IDENTITY CODE: SW_MATURITY_2
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 2 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Carnegie Mellon's Software Engineering Institute (SEI) created the CMMI model to evaluate the process maturity of software development organizations and to guide organizations toward higher levels of effectiveness. This competency will explore the key concepts of CMMI as well as the broader goal of continuous improvement, in order to prepare students to lead development efforts.
