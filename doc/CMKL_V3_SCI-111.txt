Details for Competency SCI-111 (Light and Optics (Physics II))

IDENTITY CODE: OPTICS_4
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 4 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
This course explores the fundamental principles of light and optics, providing a comprehensive understanding of how light behaves and interacts with matter. Students will delve into topics such as geometrical optics, wave optics, polarization, diffraction, interference, and optical instruments. Through theoretical study and practical experiments, learners will investigate the properties of light, including reflection, refraction, dispersion, and the formation of images by lenses and mirrors.
