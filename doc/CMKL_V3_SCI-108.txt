Details for Competency SCI-108 (Thermodynamics (Physics I))

IDENTITY CODE: THERMODYN_3
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 3 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Thermodynamics is the study of energy and its transformations. This foundational course explores the principles governing heat, work, and energy transfer within engineering systems. Students will delve into the laws of thermodynamics, including concepts such as entropy, temperature, and the relationships between different forms of energy
