Details for Competency HCD-303 (Engineered Arts: AI for Gaming)

IDENTITY CODE: ENG_ARTS_AI_GAME_6
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 6 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Game development can incorporate AI from various perspectives:
AI tools are increasingly refined from procedural content generation, asset creation, NPC behaviors, or interactive simulations to become key components of modern game development practices.
This course will explore the most popular tools integrated, or not, with Unreal 5.
