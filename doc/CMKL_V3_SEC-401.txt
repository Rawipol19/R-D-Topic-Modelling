Details for Competency SEC-401 (Privacy Attacks)

IDENTITY CODE: PRIVACY_ATTACKS_2
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 2 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Privacy becomes a main target for exploiting personal data. Privacy attacks aim to make use of personal data to cause any harmful activities to its owner. In this competency, you will learn about various privacy attacks, including de-anonymization attacks, membership inference attacks, etc.
