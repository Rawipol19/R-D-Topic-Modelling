Details for Competency SCI-105 (Kinematics: describe motion (Physis I))

IDENTITY CODE: KINEMATICS_3
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 3 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
The Kinematics: Describing Motion course provides an introduction to the fundamental principles and concepts of kinematics, the branch of mechanics that deals with the motion of objects. This course covers topics such as displacement, velocity, acceleration, and the equations of motion.
