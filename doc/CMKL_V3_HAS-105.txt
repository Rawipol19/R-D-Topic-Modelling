Details for Competency HAS-105 (Global Histories)

IDENTITY CODE: GLOBAL_HISTORIES_9
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 9 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
This course provides a comprehensive exploration of global historical events, movements, and processes from ancient times to the present. Students will engage with foundational historical theories and methodologies to analyze and compare significant historical phenomena across different regions and periods. Topics include the study of empires and colonialism, revolutions and social movements, and global trade networks and cultural exchanges. Through critical evaluation and comparative analysis, students will gain a deeper understanding of how historical events have shaped the interconnected world we live in today. This course fosters critical thinking and historical awareness, preparing students to engage with complex global historical issues.
