Details for Competency HAS-109 (Ethics and Policy Issues)

IDENTITY CODE: ETHICS_POLICY_2
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 2 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
This competency provides an exploration of the intersection between ethics and public policy, focusing on how ethical theories and frameworks can be applied to analyze and resolve policy issues. Students will engage with foundational ethical theories and methodologies to evaluate contemporary policy debates and their moral implications. 
RESPONSIBLE INSTRUCTOR: UNSPECIFIED
PREREQUISITES
None
