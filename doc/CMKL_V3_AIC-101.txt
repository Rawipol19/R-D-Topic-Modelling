Details for Competency AIC-101 (Introduction to Artificial Intelligence)

IDENTITY CODE: AI_INTRO_2
REQUIRED? true
CREDITS: 2 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
This competency considers the definition of artificial intelligence and provides an overview of AI methods and applications. It also gives students a brief introduction to the history of AI. We discuss the different approaches to AI with reference to the AI Core pillar in AiCE. Finally we conclude by considering the many societal issues raised by the modern proliferation of AI.
