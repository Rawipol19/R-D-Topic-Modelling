DESCRIPTION
Open Source Software is software that is developed by a community of volunteers and made available, with its source code, for free. OSS has become very important to support continued advances in computing. Thus the quality of OSS is critically important. However, OSS development processes are distinctly different from those followed in formally constituted development teams.
