Details for Competency HCD-404 (Application of Game Engine & Project Prototyping)

IDENTITY CODE: GAME_PROTO_6
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 6 (Graded)
DESCRIPTION
Application of Game Engine and Project Prototyping is a special course dedicated to pushing the limit of Design, Tech, and Artistic execution of one of your current Game/Experience/Capstone prototypes. 
This is a predominantly a lab-based competency.
The prerequisites for this course are the "Game Design", and "Game Engine Fundamentals" courses.

Your Prototype will be on Unreal 5.
RESPONSIBLE INSTRUCTOR: Lorenzo Avi

   -- HCD-301 - Game Engine Fundamentals
