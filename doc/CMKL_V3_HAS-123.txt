Details for Competency HAS-123 (AI and Computer Engineering for Community Impact II)

IDENTITY CODE: AI_COMMUNITY_2_4
REQUIRED? false
CREDITS: 4 (Pass/Fail)
DESCRIPTION
This competency offers students a unique opportunity to apply their knowledge of AI and computer engineering in real-world community settings. Throughout the time, students will engage in hands-on projects and community service activities designed to address specific needs within local communities. The course emphasizes experiential learning, fostering practical skills, social responsibility, and a deeper understanding of the societal impacts of technology.
Term: Spring (during Winter Break)
Location: Varied (local community sites)
Format: On-site, activities-based, no lecture
